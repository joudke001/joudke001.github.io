Plants and Trees

Seeing plants around the city, especially when flowers appear, gives both color and a brighter feel to the area. So, it is refreshing to see them wherever you are. Something with such visual vibrance shouldn't just be reserved for only the country or the suburbs.

Just like with plants, trees can be colorful, and not just in fall with the nice foliage, prior to their leaves dropping off. You can see when springtime comes, flowers appear on tree branches as well. If you pay attention, you can spot trees like these in spring time, with an abundance of flowers.
