GREEN CITY

Initiative

It's been understood that having trees and plants in the city, can promote fresh air within urban areas. Various organizations plant trees around the city do this, for a number of reasons. Some do it with the intent of bringing awareness of what trees and plants can be good for.


Having trees and plants not only promotes better air quality, they also contribute to lower air temperatures throughout the city. It also makes for better scenery, so you're not just walking past buildings and vehicles all of the time.
