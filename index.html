GREEN CITY


This is my project, where I show an appreciation of nature in an urban environment, while also observing other related issues that we see everyday.


The purpose of this project

This project was chosen to show and share a mild/moderate appreciation for nature, with a primary focus on trees, flowers and plants grown in most urban areas. What I hope to bring is a perspective of what I see regularly, as well as some things that I want to see, in regards to beautiful trees, plants and flowers, while residing in an urban environment.
